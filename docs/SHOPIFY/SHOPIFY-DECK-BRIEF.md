## Slide 5 — How it works (the tech, simply)

1. **You install Automatos** from the Shopify App Store (one click, standard Shopify OAuth)
2. **Our wizard analyses your store** — products, policies, reviews, sales data, traffic — and builds a strategic brief in under 10 minutes
3. **We seed your AI team** — 6-8 agents, each trained on your specific store data
4. **You drop widgets into your theme** — 2-3 lines of code per widget, or we do it for you
5. **Your agents run autonomously** — they learn, remember, and improve over time

The widgets are Shadow DOM isolated (they can't break your theme), zero-dependency (no bloat), and stream responses in real time (no loading spinners).
